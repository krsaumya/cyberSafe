India-Specific Cybersecurity Laws

IT Act 2000 & IPC Basics

The Information Technology Act 2000 is India's primary law dealing with cybercrime and e-commerce. It defines cyber offenses and prescribes penalties for cybercrimes.

Minor Offenses

Typically involve fines and short-term imprisonment (up to 3 years)

Serious Offenses

May involve longer imprisonment terms and substantial penalties
